Activities

What We Do at the Shed

Whether you want to learn something new, share your skills, or just have a chat over a cuppa — there's a place for you here.

Woodworking & Crafts

Get hands-on with timber projects, from small repairs to custom builds. All skill levels welcome — our experienced members are happy to help.

Social Connection

Sometimes the most important work is just showing up. The shed is a place to be around other blokes, have a laugh, and feel part of a community.

Skill Sharing

Got a skill? Teach it. Want to learn? We've got someone who can help. From carpentry to whittling, the knowledge in the shed is extraordinary.

Community Projects

We take on projects that benefit the local community — building furniture, fixing equipment, and contributing to City of Prospect initiatives.

Morning Tea & Catch Up

Every session includes a proper morning tea. It's where friendships are built and where the best conversations happen.

Mentorship

Older members pass on trades knowledge to younger men. Real skills, real experience — the kind of mentorship that makes a lasting difference.

About Us

A Place to Belong in Collinswood

Part of the Prospect community since the beginning

Prospect Mens Shed is a community-run organisation based at 31 D'Erlanger Ave, Collinswood SA 5081. We are proudly funded by the City of Prospect and exist to improve the health, wellbeing, and social connection of men in our local area.

Our shed is fully wheelchair accessible and designed to be genuinely inclusive. We believe that every man deserves a place to belong.

3 Days a Week

Open Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday from 10am to 2pm. Drop in whenever suits you.

CHSP Members Welcome

Prospect Mens Shed is currently accepting CHSP members. If you are eligible, we would love to have you join us.

Free to Join

Free to join. Proudly funded by the City of Prospect as part of their community wellbeing programme.

How to Join — It Could Not Be Simpler

Step 1: Come in and say hello

No booking needed. No forms to fill in. Just walk through the door during opening hours — Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday between 10am and 2pm.

Step 2: Meet the blokes

We'll put the kettle on. Have a cuppa, see what's on, have a look around the shed. No pressure to commit to anything.

Step 3: Start participating

Get involved at your own pace. Join a project, share a skill, or just come in for the conversation. The shed works at your rhythm.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question before you come in? We've answered the most common ones below.

Do I need woodworking experience?

Not at all. Beginners are just as welcome as experienced tradespeople. Whatever your skill level, there is something for you at the shed. Our members love sharing what they know.

Is it really free?

Free to join. The shed is funded by the City of Prospect as part of their community wellbeing investment. There are no joining fees or hidden costs.

Is the shed wheelchair accessible?

Yes. The shed is fully wheelchair accessible. We are committed to being genuinely inclusive and welcoming to all men regardless of mobility.

Who can come to the shed?

Prospect Mens Shed is currently open to CHSP members. If you are a CHSP client, you are very welcome to come along.
